Our Story

Born to Preserve & Elevate Culture

Founded in 2009, Ethiopiawinet Dance Crew has grown into one of Ethiopia's most celebrated performing arts groups. We are six artists united by a shared mission: to keep the living traditions of our heritage vibrant, visible, and celebrated worldwide.

From winning national competitions including the prestigious Balgeru Idol to performing alongside Ethiopia's most popular singers in music videos and representing Ethiopia at the FIFA World Cup in Qatar 2022, Ethiopiawinet has become a defining voice in East African cultural performance.

Grounded in the rich traditions of Ethiopia and Eritrea — we bring authenticity, precision, and soul to every stage we grace.
