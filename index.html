ETHIOPIAWINET
Our Story

A Crew Born to Preserve & Elevate Culture

Founded in 2009, Ethiopiawinet Cultural Dance Crew has grown into one of Ethiopia's most celebrated performing arts groups. We are six artists united by a shared mission: to keep the living traditions of our heritage vibrant, visible, and celebrated.

From winning national competitions including the prestigious Balgeru Idol to performing alongside Ethiopia's most popular singers in music videos, Ethiopiawinet has become a defining voice in East African cultural performance.

Exposed to diverse global dance forms, we remain deeply grounded in the rich traditions of Ethiopia and Eritrea — bringing authenticity, precision, and soul to every stage we grace.

What We Do

The Art We Bring to Life

Every performance by Ethiopiawinet is a complete cultural experience — weaving together movement, sound, story, and spirit drawn from centuries of East African tradition.

💃

Traditional Dance

Authentic choreography from diverse Ethiopian and Eritrean regional traditions, performed with precision and cultural integrity.

🥁

Live Drumming

Powerful percussion rooted in Ethiopian rhythmic tradition, setting the heartbeat of every performance.

🎶

Traditional Song

Vocal performances drawing from Ethiopia's rich musical heritage, sung in indigenous languages with deep cultural meaning.

Narrative performance blending dance, music, and dramatic storytelling to bring Ethiopian history and folklore to life.
